Gigi Tibi

Our Founder

Daughter of Bicol, Advocate of Kindness, the Planet and the Education of Indigenous Youth, Veteran Life Coach, Forest Builder, Philippine Marketing Communications Industry Leader, Meditation Teacher, Student of Life.

Tracing her roots in Iriga, Bicol, Gigi Tibi is an advocate for kindness, the planet, and the education of indigenous youth. A veteran life coach, forest builder, and student of life, she blends heart and purpose in everything she does.

A graduate of the University of the Philippines in Communication and Masters in Business Administration, Gigi founded RadManila Communications, a creative agency grounded in compassion, joy and excellence.

Her love for learning led her to start Yabá—originally a micro-library under a tree. After a typhoon destroyed it, she transformed it into the Yabá Educational Foundation, devoted to empowering Aeta children through education. Since 2017, Yabá has helped produce teachers, engineers, and community leaders.

Gigi with the help of her family and friends, also helped grow a forest of 2,000 indigenous trees at the foot Mt. Iriga, her act of gratitude to the Earth. As a meditation teacher, she shares her belief that “World peace begins with befriending one’s mind.”
